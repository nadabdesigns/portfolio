Nadia Batchelor

About

Nadia Batchelor is a Brooklyn-based multidisciplinary designer crafting brand systems, visual identities, and digital experiences. At Notorious111, she supports scalable branding initiatives. Her independent work engages conversations surrounding the Black diaspora, accessibility, and identity, positioning design as both a cultural framework and connective force. She holds a Master of Professional Studies in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design, where she studied under AIGA Gold Medalist Lucille Tenazas.
